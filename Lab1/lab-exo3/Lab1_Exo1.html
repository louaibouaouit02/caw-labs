Brief History

Ottoman Rule (1517–1917): Palestine remained under Ottoman control for 400 years, where Muslims, Christians, and Jews lived together.

1948 – Nakba (Catastrophe): : Israel declared independence; hundreds of thousands of Palestinians were expelled or fled their homes. The West Bank came under Jordanian control and Gaza under Egyptian rule.

1967 – Six-Day War: Israel occupied the West Bank, Gaza, East Jerusalem, and other Arab territories.
