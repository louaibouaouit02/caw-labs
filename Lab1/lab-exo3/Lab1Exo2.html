Between 2023 and 2025, the Israeli aggression on Gaza killed over 64,000 people and injured more than 161,000,according to the Palestinian Ministry of Health.

Among the victims were 1,009 infants, with 28 children, 14 women, and 7 killed on average each day. Most of the murdered were children and young men, showing the devastating impact on civilians.
